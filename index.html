Account & Data Deletion

Learn how to safely delete your Maratha Business Hub (MBH) account and remove your personal and business records from our databases.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent! Once your account is deleted, all registered businesses, profile images, job listings, and community events associated with your account will be permanently erased. This data cannot be recovered.

How to Request Account Deletion

We provide two simple and secure methods to delete your account and associated data:

  1. Method A: In-App Deletion Request (Recommended)

    Open the Maratha Business Hub app on your device, log in, navigate to **Profile > Settings**, and tap on the **"Delete Account"** option. Confirm your request, and your account will be deleted instantly.

  2. Method B: Email Request (Direct Administration Support)

    If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access your account, you can request account deletion by emailing our admin team at aniketatkare@gmail.com. Please write "Account Deletion Request - Maratha Business Hub" as the subject, and specify your registered email address and name. We will permanently delete your account within 7 business days.

Data Retention Policy (What is Deleted vs. Kept)

We respect your privacy and do not retain any of your personal information once you choose to leave our platform.

Data Category Status After Deletion Retention Period
Authentication Profile (Name, Email, Password, Phone Number) Permanently Deleted Immediate (in-app) or within 7 days (email)
Business Listing Profile (Business name, address, description, photos, WhatsApp) Permanently Deleted Immediate (in-app) or within 7 days (email)
Job Postings (Open/Closed job vacancies) Permanently Deleted Immediate (in-app) or within 7 days (email)
Event Listings (Community and business programs) Permanently Deleted Immediate (in-app) or within 7 days (email)
Visitor Logs & Analytics (Profile visit histories) Permanently Deleted Immediate (in-app) or within 7 days (email)
